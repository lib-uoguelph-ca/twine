Each day, we make choices that can affect our world. The ripple effect describes the phenomenon that each one of our decisions and actions have its own effect on the world around us. We are not defined by these decisions, but it can be beneficial to be aware of the choices we make and the consequences that they may lead to. This game will take you to a hypothetical world where each choice changes the next (just like our own world). You control the world's fate! You will make decisions that will lead our world into different directions. You don't like your result, play again. Or if you want to see the alternative option, play again and change up your answers. This is your opportunity to create a future that you would want to live in. What would you do if you were in control? Have fun, but use what you may learn here and apply it to your own life. Oh, and I hope you're ready for time travel!
